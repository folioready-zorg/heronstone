Our Story

A passion for water, stone, and the landscapes of Melbourne.

The Craft of Water

Heronstone was founded on a simple belief: that water has the power to transform a space. A well-designed pond or water feature doesn't just add visual interest — it creates an atmosphere, a sense of calm, a connection to the natural world.

Based in Melbourne, we specialise exclusively in the design and construction of bespoke water features. From tranquil garden ponds to expansive natural swimming pools, each project is approached with the same dedication to craft, quality, and lasting beauty.

We work closely with homeowners, landscape architects, and builders to ensure every installation is perfectly integrated into its setting — not an afterthought, but a centrepiece.

Our Philosophy

Why we do things differently

Bespoke Only

We don't do cookie-cutter installations. Every project is designed from scratch to suit the specific landscape, architecture, and desires of our clients. No templates, no shortcuts.

Built to Last

We use premium materials and proven construction methods. Our water features are engineered to endure Melbourne's climate and provide decades of enjoyment with minimal intervention.

Natural Harmony

Water features should feel like they belong. We draw on natural forms, native plantings, and local stone to create installations that complement rather than compete with the landscape.
