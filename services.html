Our Services

From initial concept through to ongoing care — every aspect of your water feature, handled with expertise.

What We Do

Each service is tailored to your property and preferences

Pond Design Photo

Bespoke Pond Design & Construction

Our signature service. We design and build custom ponds that become the defining feature of your outdoor space. From intimate courtyard water gardens to large-scale estate ponds, every element — shape, depth, edging, planting — is considered to create something truly unique.

We handle every stage: site assessment, design, excavation, lining, filtration, planting, and finishing. The result is a pond that looks as though it's always been there.

Natural Pool Photo

Natural Swimming Pools

Chemical-free swimming in your own garden. Our natural swimming pools use biological filtration and regeneration zones planted with aquatic species to keep water crystal clear — no chlorine, no salt, no harsh chemicals.

They're beautiful year-round, functioning as a living water feature even when you're not swimming. We design each pool to integrate naturally with the surrounding landscape.

Water Feature Photo

Water Features & Fountains

From cascading rock walls to minimalist spouts and blade falls, we create water features that bring movement and sound to your garden. Whether you want a dramatic focal point or a subtle background murmur, we'll design something that fits.

Our water features are built with quality pumps, plumbing, and stone to ensure reliable, low-maintenance operation for years to come.

Koi Pond Photo

Koi Ponds & Aquatic Ecosystems

Specialist koi ponds built to support healthy fish populations. We design with proper depth, filtration, aeration, and biosecurity in mind — creating not just a pond, but a thriving aquatic ecosystem.

Our koi pond installations include bottom drains, multi-stage filtration, UV clarifiers, and all the infrastructure needed for a healthy, low-maintenance koi habitat.

Maintenance Photo

Maintenance & Restoration

Keep your water feature performing beautifully with our tailored maintenance programs. We offer seasonal servicing, water quality management, equipment checks, planting refresh, and general upkeep.

We also restore and renovate ageing or neglected water features — whether it's one of ours or one that needs new life. If it involves water in a garden, we can help.
